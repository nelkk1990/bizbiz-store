No. All templates work with a free Canva account. Simply click the template link, create a free Canva account (if you don't have one), and you'll have a fully editable copy.
Can I edit colours, fonts, and text?
Yes — everything is editable. Change colours, fonts, photos, and all text directly in Canva. You get your own editable copy, not a locked file.

Due to the digital nature of the products, we offer refunds only if the template link is non-functional. Please preview the template using the "Preview →" link before purchasing.
